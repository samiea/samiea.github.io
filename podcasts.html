MKUltra Podcast

Together with Brendan, George, Harrison, Kyle, and Justin, we host an investigative podcast touching on the operations of MKUltra, one of the longest standing conspiracy theories on a covert CIA project with questionable morality. As any good conversation about government ethics would, we have to talk about a story that relates to both of stranger things and bourne ultimatum. Together, we analyze the content behind one of perhaps the most infamous and questionable experimentations done by the CIA.
